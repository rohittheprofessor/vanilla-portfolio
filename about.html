ABOUT ME

Dedication to Computational Excellence

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science with strong interests in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Remote Sensing applications.

Currently pursuing my Ph.D., my research focuses on deep learning-based permafrost mapping using satellite data. I aim to bridge theoretical computation with real-world environmental challenges.

I actively mentor students, contribute to research publications, and engage in academic collaborations.
